# Sr. Manager – Financial Systems and ITBP / Senior Manager – Accounting Systems

**Location:** Denver, Colorado

## Summary

This position will function as a key leader of the Financial Systems Center of Excellence as new technologies emerge and existing systems evolve to better serve the internal and external stakeholders. The key responsibility will be to help improve operational efficiency, maintain balance sheet integrity, cut time to close, and safeguard compliance and regulatory requirements. The incumbent will be responsible for defining and designing best-in-class system solutions in accordance with GAAP and IFRS accounting practices by performing feasibility analyses with a team of analysts and system managers.

This position will also function as a key leader of a functional center of excellence as new technologies emerge and existing systems evolve. The key responsibility will be to help improve operational efficiency, maintain balance sheet integrity, cut time to close, and safeguard compliance and regulatory requirements. The incumbent will be responsible for defining and designing best-in-class processes and financial system models by performing feasibility analyses and managing a team of analysts and system managers.

## Essential Functions

- Reports to the SVP & Finance Business Partner – Global Operations; has 1–5 direct reports and up to 5 indirect reports.
- Requires up to 25% travel (international/domestic), including overnight stays.
- Flexible schedule with the ability to work remotely.
- Partner with business to analyze and solve complex financial problems.
- Understand and align with the strategic direction set by executives; clearly communicate team goals, deliverables, and keep the team updated on changes.
- Oversee IT/Finance projects: identify tasks, determine effort, manage resources, and report progress to management.
- Drive new solutions, leveraging and, where needed, adapting existing methods and procedures.
- Coordinate process improvement discussions with business leaders.
- Interact with peers and/or management levels to define functional strategies.
- Collaborate with Global Value Stream Owners and Solution Design Leads to support RTR rollout and assess localization requirements of the ERP solution for in-scope business units.
- Support timely decision-making and manage escalations for financial decisions.
- Actively educate business process owners to drive solution optimization, simplification, and standardization capabilities.
- Coordinate setup of a functional center of excellence around RTR.
- Manage, coordinate, and collaborate with relevant scrum, delivery, deployment, and project leadership teams to implement and deploy agile methodology.
- Participate in key sprint planning, retrospectives, and other key ceremonies for global initiatives across technology platforms.
- Along with the scrum team, identify, monitor, and implement key agreed-upon improvements.
- Facilitate the design and definition of the accounting system configuration to meet financial/managerial reporting requirements.
- Serve as a key decision maker for system configuration changes impacting finance/accounting operations and the reporting model.
- Ensure proper adaptation of functional requirements into the global core model.
- Collaborate with finance/accounting teams to define finance data structures, business rules, processes, and functions for their operations.
- Work with other departments, including Finance and Accounting, to review business and system requirements and processes; plan for build, test, and post-implementation support of new systems.
- Identify new opportunities, fill gaps, and improve internal operations to scale quickly.

## Required Qualifications (Education, Experience, Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities)

- Bachelor’s degree (preferred) with 9+ years of relevant accounting experience; or Associate’s degree with 11+ years of relevant experience; or 13+ years of relevant experience.
- 7+ years of experience configuring, deploying, and managing ERP projects across Accounting and Finance functions.
- 4+ years of experience working in finance or accounting departments.
- Demonstrated ability to effectively communicate with all levels of the organization.
- Strong influencing, facilitating, and consulting skills in working with stakeholders at all levels and across departments.
